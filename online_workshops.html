Kickstarter Workshop

Save the date! Sept 8 & 9, 2021

The goals of this workshop are to brainstorm ideas for kickstarter research projects, foster collaborations, help new members to get involved, and develop proposals for the Kickstarter Grants program.

Sessions at the workshop will around 2hrs long, and distributed in time to allow members in different timezones to join.

Workshop sign-up

There will be no registration fee or deadline for this workshop, but if you would like to attend, please use this form to let us know for planning purposes!
