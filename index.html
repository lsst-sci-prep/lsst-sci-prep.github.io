Preparing for Astrophysics with LSST is a new research and community-building initiative for members of three participating Rubin Science Collaborations: Transients and Variable Stars (TVS), Stars, Milky Way and Local Volume (SMWLV) and the Solar System Science Collaboration (SSSC).
